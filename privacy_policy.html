Turf&Ardor

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Turf&Ardor ("we," "our," or "the App") is a social and dating application built for the FIFA World Cup 2026. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Turf&Ardor, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, date of birth, and password.
  • Profile information: Photos, nationality, supported team, bio, languages spoken, interests, and location preferences.
  • Usage data: Swipe actions, matches, messages sent and received, and app navigation behavior.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Location data: Approximate location based on your selected host city (we do not use GPS tracking).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account.
  • Calculate match compatibility scores with other users.
  • Enable messaging between matched users.
  • Provide World Cup hub features (schedules, city guides, safety info).
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs.
  • Send important notifications about your matches and messages.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data with:

  • Supabase: Our backend infrastructure provider, which stores your account and profile data securely.
  • Apple: For app distribution and push notification delivery.
  • Law enforcement: Only when required by law or to protect user safety.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure with row-level security policies. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time through the app.
  • Edit or update your profile information.
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications.

7. Data Retention

Turf&Ardor is designed for the World Cup 2026 window (June 11 — July 19, 2026). After the tournament concludes, we will retain your data for 90 days to allow you to export any conversations or connections. After this period, all personal data will be permanently deleted unless you request earlier deletion.

8. Age Requirement

Turf&Ardor is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will delete their account and data immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through in-app notifications. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
